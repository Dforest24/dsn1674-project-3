us

Opened in 1942, at 290 Dalhousie Street, Mellos has become a staple in the Byward Market. All of our staff our family and will always make you feel at home. Whether you’re looking for a classic breakfast/brunch or to spice it up with our dinner menu, we have something for you. Turning your old favourites into a new favourite.

brunch

We’ve been serving essentially the same brunch since we opened and don’t plan on stopping. Eggs, meat, home fries and toast. “Just one meat? No, honey it comes with all the meat.”

dinner

Our after-6 menu has completely changed over the last two years with our new owner. We have all the classics, done like no one else does them; we have liquor and beer, all at a fair price. The menu is constantly changing to suit what is freshly available and the season. Our staple burger is cooked to however you chose (we like it red) and served on a grilled cheese bread. Our fried chicken is served on a bed of brussel sprouts and a chilli spiced pulled pork. Our goal is to take every dish that’s available everywhere and add our personal twist to it.
